Specialist hotel fit-out and interior design contractor founded 2001 in Madrid by Jorge Merino — one of Spain's first project management firms focused on hotel equipment and renovation. Delivers turnkey interior design, purchasing management and renovation for hotels from new-build to refurbishment. Six offices across Europe (Madrid, Warsaw), Africa (Casablanca), and Latin America (Medellín, Lima, Miami). Notable clients include Golden Tulip Hotels and multiple 4 and 5-star properties across Spain, Latin America and North Africa.
