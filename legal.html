FitOut Post is the data controller responsible for personal information collected through this platform. We can be reached at privacy@fitoutpost.com.

2. What we collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Contact form submissions — name, email address, and the message you send us.

FitOut Post is a read-only platform. We do not operate user accounts or require registration to access the news feed or company directory.

3. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

Where the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 applies, we process personal data on the following lawful bases:

  • Legitimate interests — for analytics and platform security.

We use collected information to:
