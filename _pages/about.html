About FitOut Post

The world's fit-out industry in one place

FitOut Post is an independent intelligence platform dedicated to the global interior fit-out industry — the $290bn sector that transforms shells into workplaces, hotels, retail stores, hospitals, and hospitality spaces.

Our mission

The fit-out industry is fragmented, multilingual, and badly served by generalist construction media. A hotel interior project in Riyadh, a commercial tenant improvement in Chicago, and a retail rollout in Tokyo share the same supply chain dynamics — but nobody was connecting those dots globally.

FitOut Post aggregates news, tenders, and project pipeline signals from every continent into a single, structured feed — updated every day before 08:00 — so that contractors, designers, developers, suppliers, and investors can find the intelligence they need without the noise.

Editorial standards

  • 1
    No editorial bias. We do not accept payment for editorial coverage. Our news feed is algorithmically ranked by relevance and recency, not commercial relationships.
  • 2
    Primary sources only. We link directly to original reporting — publisher websites, official procurement portals, and company announcements. We do not republish full articles.
  • 3
    Geographic precision. Every item is tagged by country and continent using structured data. We correct misclassifications as they are identified.
  • 4
    Tender accuracy. Tender notices are sourced from TED (EU), national procurement portals, and government procurement feeds. We flag expired tenders and remove them at the next daily refresh.
  • 5
    Corrections policy. We publish corrections promptly. If you identify an error, contact us.

Data sources

FitOut Post aggregates from over 200 identified sources including: trade press (FX, Sleeper, FRAME, Mixology News, Hospitality Design, Contract Journal), Google News in 14 languages, TED European procurement database, national e-procurement portals, company press releases, LinkedIn announcements, and project award databases.

Data is refreshed daily. The platform is built on a static architecture — every page is pre-built from JSON data files — ensuring fast, reliable delivery without server infrastructure.
